Follow the Yellow Brick Road
December 30, 2015

This fall I took 16.31 Feedback and Control Systems as I have decided to pursue a minor in Aero/Astro! In the course we received a Rolling Spider Parrot drone and learned different methods to control it. During the labs we implemented these controllers.

Our final project was open ended. My group decided to make the drone use its onboard camera to follow a yellow line on the ground. Unfortunately in later versions we forgot the yellow tape and had to make use of the more common electrical tape.

The code for our project is available online at github.
